About us

A Look into your engine's story.

OilVue's early detection gives you the information to help preserve your asset's health.

Our easy-to-understand reporting shows the health of your engine, health of your engine's oil, and where to focus your maintenance efforts.

Our Core Features

  • Wear Metals

    Microscopic particles in your engine's oil can diagnose possible component issues. OilVue analysts use our extensive sample library to help detect premature component wear.

  • Contaminants

    Contaminants such as coolant can determine possible leaks that may lead to engine failure.

  • Physical Chemical Tests

    Physical and Chemical Tests help determine the health of your oil. Get the most out of your oil and safely extend time between oil changes.
